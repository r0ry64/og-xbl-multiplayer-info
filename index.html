Original Xbox live multiplayer info (Insignia supported games only)

**Work in progress** Last update: 2025-05-30

Legend

Join in Progress Supports joining a multiplayer lobby while the match is in progress, either as a spectator or player.
Voice Chat Type Global: Talk and listen to everyone in the lobby. Some games put all dead players and spectators in the same voice channel, separate from global or team channels.
Team: Talk and listen to your teammates only.
Push-to-talk: Push and hold a specific button to talk.
Proximity: Talk and listen to players near you only.
Port Forwarding Although it is generally recommended to port forward your Xbox (Port 3074 UDP) for the best experience on Insignia, some games require it more than others.
DLC Price Free: All DLC for this game was free, and is now provided by Insignia. Some games may have both free and paid DLC, Insignia will only be providing the free DLC.
Paid: All DLC for this game was paid, and will not be provided by Insignia.
Netcode: Ok: May experience lag/delay, rubberbanding/jittery gameplay, collision glitches. Mostly noticable on higher pings. Still very playable.
Bad: Very noticable lag, slow input delay, frame delay, noticable host advantage. May be unplayable on higher pings.
